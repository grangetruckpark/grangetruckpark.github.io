About Us



Opened in 2019 having gained TAPA Approved Secure Parking Operator status, Grange Truck Park in South Yorkshire offers high security parking for hauliers carrying high value cargo.

The Site has a heavy grade, sliding steel security gate and full perimeter fencing, all monitoried by a Home Office approved

CCTV system including motion tracking technology which continuously detects threats both inside and outside of the facility.

The whole site is covered by motion sensors and a professional monitoring team is on standby 24/7 to respond to any alarm.

As well as the high level of security offered at Grange Truck Park, the site also includes toilet and shower facilities for both male and female drivers.
